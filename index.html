My name is Alireza Karduni.

I am a researcher in Human Computer Interaction, Data Visualization, and Computational Social / Urban Sciences.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Scholar jointly working at Northwestern University's Departments of Computer Science and Psychology. I study how humans interact with information on social media from two lenses of social / urban interactions and individual judgements. I also create tools and visualizations that can potentially help humans make better decisions in a variety of tasks. My research is interdisciplinary and connects human-computer interaction, cognitive sciences, and urban / social sciences.
